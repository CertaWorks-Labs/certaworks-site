Privacy Policy

CertaWorks Labs LLC (DBA: CertaWorks)  ·  certaworks.dev  ·  Last updated: May 2026

1. What we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide when you submit a beta interest form on certaworks.dev. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company or project name (optional)
  • A description of the agents or tools you use (optional)

We do not use analytics platforms, tracking pixels, fingerprinting scripts, or advertising networks. We do not set cookies beyond what is strictly necessary for the page to function. We do not collect IP addresses for the purpose of identifying or profiling you.

2. How we use your information

Information you submit via the beta interest form is used solely to:

  • Notify you when the hosted beta becomes available
  • Send occasional product updates relevant to what you signed up for
  • Respond to any direct questions you include in your submission

We will not send you promotional messages unrelated to CertaWorks products, and we will not add you to any marketing list without your knowledge.

3. Data storage and security

Form submissions are stored with reasonable security measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data. We are a small, early-stage company and do not operate large-scale data infrastructure. Your information is stored in limited, access-controlled systems and is not retained beyond the period needed to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take data protection seriously, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Ever.

We may use minimal third-party services to operate the site (for example, a hosting provider or form handler). These services are bound to handle data only as directed by us and are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes.

CertaWorks products — the 10 local MCP server tools available at certaworks.dev — operate locally on your own infrastructure. They do not transmit data to CertaWorks servers. Your agent data stays with you.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your personal information
  • Withdrawal — withdraw consent and unsubscribe from communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at blair.hall@certaworks.dev. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and at no cost to you.

6. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this privacy policy or the personal information we hold, please contact:

7. Updates to this policy

We may update this privacy policy as our products evolve or as legal requirements change. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes that affect how we handle your personal data will be communicated directly to beta list subscribers by email.

Continued use of certaworks.dev after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
